## Reading Journey Guide Upgrade 2.0
- Rebuilt the book guide around a P3-friendly five-step routine: Read, Think, Notice, Explain, Write.
- Added CORE / TALK / CHALLENGE levels, clear stop rules, read-aloud coaching, and parent prompts.
- Added a Wonder Chapters 1–5 reading map with chapter-specific skills, questions, sentence patterns, and writing outcomes.
- Expanded chapter records with prediction, one-sentence summary, evidence, reading skill, task level, and an optional challenge response.
- Preserved existing chapter, vocabulary, sentence-bank, progress, backup, and Google Drive data structures.
